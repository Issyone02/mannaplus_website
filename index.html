Who We Are

About Mannaplus

Integrating academic rigour with spiritual and moral upbringing — a legacy of educational transformation since 2012.

Our Philosophy

At Mannaplus, every child is unique. We blend the Nigerian national curriculum with international best practices, emphasising critical thinking, creativity, and leadership development.

Our safe, nurturing environment develops learners who excel academically and shine in character, faith, and community contribution.

Why Choose Mannaplus?

  • Certified, passionate, and experienced teachers
  • Smart classrooms equipped with STEM labs
  • Sports, arts, drama & leadership clubs
  • Pastoral care, counselling & mentoring
  • Modern library & e-learning resources
  • Robotics and coding for innovators
  • Dedicated examination preparation centres

Secondary School

🏫 College — JSS1 to SSS3

Preparing future-ready graduates for WASSCE, NECO, JAMB, and global opportunities with excellence at every level.

Science & Technology

State-of-the-art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science. Our robotics and coding clubs empower the next generation of innovators.

Hands-on experiments, research projects, and STEM fairs keep students engaged and industry-ready from an early age.

Commercial & Arts

Diverse subject offerings including Accounting, Commerce, Literature, Government, Economics, and Creative Writing. Career counselling is integrated across all years.

Our commercial track produces graduates ready for university, entrepreneurship, and professional careers.

Leadership Campus

Student council, debate team, Model UN, and entrepreneurship fairs forge tomorrow's leaders today.

Exam Excellence
